¿What are QR Codes?

The QR codes (in English, QR Code) are a type of two-dimensional barcodes. Unlike a conventional barcode (such as EAN-13, Code 3 of 9, UPC), the information is encoded within a square, allowing a large amount of alphanumeric information to be stored.

QR codes are easily identifiable by their square shape and by the three squares located in the top corners and the bottom left corner.
